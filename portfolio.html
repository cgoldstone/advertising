Recent Projects:

BRASSO

What is Brasso?

  • Metal polish
  • First manufactured in 1905
  • Also works for removing scratches from CDs, DVDs, watches, and LEGOs

Who is Brasso?

  • Market position: "Tried and true" metal polish
  • Relevant
  • Target market: Traditional people; 18+; male and female, all races and income levels; not concerned with being "trendy"

Marketing Objective

  • Capture brand personality
  • Make Brasso relevant for younger users
  • Maintain Brasso's century-old heritage
  • Ultimately: revive the brand

*In collaboration with Rene Baboir*

Current Project:

St. Louis City Museum

What is the City Museum?

  • Museum consisting largely of repurposed architectural and industrial objects
  • Visitors are encouraged to touch, climb on, and play with the exhibits
  • Opened in 1997 by artist Bob Cassilly and his then-wife Gail Cassilly

Who is the City Museum

  • Fun and whimsical
  • Youthful and adventurous
  • Market Position: unique amusement center that is a mix of a playground and modern art exhibit
  • Target Market: teenagers/young adults (currently in school/college); parents (with one or more young children, 4-13); residents of St. Louis or tourists

Marketing Objective

  • Increase awareness of City Museum among both residents and visitors to St. Louis
  • Differentiate the City Museum from other St. Louis attractions
  • Increase appeal among adults

*In collaboration with Chan Kwon*
